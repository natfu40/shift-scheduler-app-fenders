# 👤 Employee User Guide

Welcome to the Fenders Brewing Scheduler! This guide will help you navigate and use the system as a regular employee.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### First-Time Login

1. **Receive Login Credentials**
   - Your administrator will create your account and provide your email and temporary password
   - You'll receive login credentials via your preferred communication method

2. **Initial Login Process**
   - Navigate to the application URL
   - Enter your email and temporary password
   - **Important**: You'll be required to change your password on first login
   - Choose a secure password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Confirm your new password

3. **Password Requirements**
   - Minimum 6 characters
   - Mix of letters, numbers, and special characters recommended
   - Should be unique and not used elsewhere

### Subsequent Logins

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login"

## 🏠 Home Page Overview

After logging in, you'll see the **Home** page with several key sections:

### Navigation Menu
- **🏠 Home**: Main dashboard (where you are now)
- **📅 Calendar**: Calendar view of shifts
- **Your Name**: Click for user menu with options:
  - **🔑 Change Password**: Update your password securely
  - **🚪 Logout**: Sign out of the system

### Main Dashboard Sections

#### 1. Your Upcoming Shifts
- Shows all your **approved** shift assignments
- Displays shift name, date/time, and description
- Only shows future shifts (today and beyond)
- Automatically sorted by date (earliest first)

#### 2. Pending Signup Requests
- Shows shifts you've signed up for but haven't been approved yet
- Includes a "Cancel" button to withdraw your request
- Useful for tracking which shifts are awaiting admin approval

#### 3. Available Shifts
- Shows all shifts you can sign up for
- Only displays shifts with available slots
- Only shows future shifts
- Each shift shows:
  - Shift name and description
  - Date and time
  - Available slots remaining
  - "Sign Up" button

## 📅 Shift Management

### Signing Up for Shifts

1. **Browse Available Shifts**
   - Scroll down to the "Available Shifts" section on your Home page
   - Review shift details including date, time, and description
   - Check how many spots are still available

2. **Sign Up Process**
   - Click the green "Sign Up" button on any available shift
   - You'll see a success message confirming your signup
   - The shift will move to your "Pending Signup Requests" section
   - Wait for admin approval

3. **After Signup**
   - Your request appears in "Pending Signup Requests"
   - You'll receive confirmation once an admin approves your request
   - Approved shifts appear in "Your Upcoming Shifts"

### Managing Your Signups

#### Canceling Pending Requests
1. Go to the "Pending Signup Requests" section
2. Find the shift you want to cancel
3. Click the red "Cancel" button
4. Confirm when prompted
5. The shift will return to the available shifts pool

#### Viewing Approved Shifts
- All approved shifts appear in "Your Upcoming Shifts"
- These cannot be cancelled directly - contact your administrator if needed
- Shows complete shift details and timing

## 📱 Calendar Integration

### Adding Shifts to Your Personal Calendar

The system provides calendar integration so you can sync your shifts with your personal calendar apps (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, etc.).

#### Option 1: Subscription URL (Recommended)
1. Click the "📅 Add Shifts to Calendar" button (top right of Home page)
2. In the modal that appears, click "Copy Subscription URL"
3. Open your calendar application
4. Add a new calendar subscription using the copied URL
5. Your shifts will automatically stay updated

**Supported Calendar Apps:**
- Google Calendar
- Apple Calendar (iOS/macOS)
- Microsoft Outlook
- Any calendar app supporting iCal subscriptions

#### Option 2: Download .ics File
1. Click the "📅 Add Shifts to Calendar" button
2. Click "Download .ics File"
3. Your browser will download a calendar file
4. Open the file with your calendar application
5. Import the events

**Note**: Downloaded files are a snapshot and won't automatically update with new shifts.

### Calendar Subscription Benefits
- **Automatic Updates**: New approved shifts appear automatically
- **Mobile Sync**: Works with your phone's calendar
- **Notifications**: Set calendar reminders for your shifts
- **Integration**: Works with your existing calendar workflow

## 🔍 Finding and Filtering Shifts

### Understanding Shift Information

Each shift displays:
- **Shift Name**: Brief identifier (e.g., "Morning Brew", "Evening Service")
- **Date & Time**: Full start and end times
- **Description**: Detailed information about the shift requirements
- **Slots**: Available positions vs. filled positions

### Shift Availability Rules
- **Future Only**: You can only sign up for shifts that haven't started
- **Open Slots**: Must have available positions remaining
- **One Signup**: You can only sign up once per shift
- **Approval Required**: All signups require admin approval

## ⚠️ Important Guidelines

### Shift Signup Etiquette
- **Sign up responsibly**: Only request shifts you can actually work
- **Cancel promptly**: If you can't work a shift you requested, cancel ASAP
- **Plan ahead**: Sign up for shifts as early as possible
- **Communicate**: Contact your admin for any special requests or conflicts

### System Limitations
- **No direct cancellation** of approved shifts - contact your administrator
- **Limited slots**: Popular shifts fill up quickly
- **Admin approval required**: All signups must be approved
- **Future shifts only**: Cannot sign up for past shifts

### Security Best Practices
- **Keep your password secure** and don't share it
- **Log out** when using shared computers
- **Update your password** if you suspect it's been compromised
- **Report issues** to your administrator immediately

## 🔐 Password Management

### Changing Your Password

You can change your password at any time for enhanced security:

1. **Access Password Change**
   - Click on your name in the top-right navigation
   - Select "🔑 Change Password" from the dropdown menu

2. **Password Change Process**
   - **Current Password**: Enter your existing password
   - **New Password**: Choose a strong password (see strength meter)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter the new password
   - **Submit**: Click "Change Password" to complete

3. **Password Requirements**
   - **Minimum 6 characters** (longer is better)
   - **Strong recommended**: Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols
   - **Different from current**: Must be different from your existing password
   - **Real-time feedback**: Strength meter shows password quality

4. **Security Features**
   - **Password strength indicator**: Visual feedback on password quality
   - **Show/hide passwords**: Toggle to verify your entries
   - **Secure transmission**: All data encrypted over HTTPS
   - **Audit logging**: Password changes are logged for security

### Password Security Tips

- **Use unique passwords**: Don't reuse passwords from other services
- **Consider a password manager**: Tools like Bitwarden, LastPass, or 1Password
- **Mix character types**: Combine letters, numbers, and special characters
- **Longer is stronger**: Aim for 12+ characters when possible
- **Regular updates**: Change passwords if you suspect compromise

## 📞 Getting Help

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### "I can't see any available shifts"
- Check if there are shifts scheduled for future dates
- Confirm you haven't already signed up (check pending requests)
- Contact your administrator - they may need to create shifts

#### "My signup disappeared"
- Check your "Your Upcoming Shifts" section - it may have been approved
- Contact your administrator if a signup request vanished unexpectedly

#### "I can't log in"
- Verify your email address is correct
- Check if Caps Lock is on
- Try resetting your password (contact administrator)
- Clear your browser cache and cookies

#### "Calendar integration isn't working"
- Make sure you copied the complete subscription URL
- Try downloading the .ics file instead
- Check your calendar app's subscription settings
- Contact technical support if issues persist

### Contacting Support

For additional help:
1. **Administrator**: Contact your shift scheduling administrator first
2. **Technical Issues**: Report bugs or technical problems through your administrator
3. **Account Issues**: Password resets and account problems require admin assistance

### Tips for Success

- **Check regularly**: New shifts may be added throughout the week
- **Be prompt**: Popular shifts get filled quickly
- **Use calendar integration**: Stay organized with personal calendar sync
- **Plan ahead**: Sign up for shifts well in advance when possible
- **Stay informed**: Watch for announcements from your administrator
